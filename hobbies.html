Jen's Hobbies

Hobby One: Family Fun Time

Jen loves doing activities with her family like dressing up as super heroes and trick-or-treating. Every Holiday, she dresses up the family for the Holiday....even if they do not want to! Jen believes in celebrating Holidays to the best way possible.

Hobby Two: Family Time

Jen loves going boating with her mom as they use the time to bond and have a fun time together. If they are not out on the boat then she loves to get a nice glass of wine and some appetizers at a few of their favorite restaurant's. Jen loves spending time with her kids and husband watching the sunset on the beaches of the gulf of Mexico!

Hobby Three: Water Sports

Jen loves spending time on the water doing things like going boating and kayaking. She will take her dog with her sometimes but will quickly realize that it was a poor choice. The Dog is not the most graceful swimmer out there. One thing that Jen likes the most about the water is the smell of the salt water and the cool breezes!
