About Me

Jennifer Trevino was born in Vero beach, Florida on June 6th, 1986. She was the youngest of three kids and the second girl. Shortly after Jennifer was born, she moved to the Northwest Florida where she attended school in Freeport, FL. After graduating High School, Jennifer enlisted in the Airforce as a Medic. She remained in the Airforce and was stationed at various Airforce bases in Alaska and Nevada. After leaving the Airforce in 2011, she began attending school to become a registered nurse with the goal of continuing to service in the medical field. In 2013, Jennifer graduated nursing school and quickly become a traveling nurse where she traveled around the country taking care of elderly people. In 2014, Jennifer began her career at her current employer, Coastal Skin Surgery and Dermatology as a registered nurse. Jennifer has remained at her current employer for going on eight years because she loves working around individuals whose she has a direct influence on how they treat their health. In 2015, Jennifer met her husband, and they welcomed their daughter, Olivia, into the world in 2016. Since then, Jennifer has continued to purse advancements in her career by earning her CPCO Compliance Officer credentials and pursing a BA in Healthcare Management from Bellevue University. Since earning her CPCO credentials, she has earned a management position within her practice, where she assumed the responsibilities of a Compliance officer. Upon accepting the new position, Jennifer has also made changes in the company compliance department which have earned her practice some of the highest accolades possible. Jennifer has almost completed her BA degree and will purse continued education to where she hopes to earn her master degree in healthcare. When Jennifer is not working, she loves to spend time with her husband, two kids, and two animals where they spend a lot of time on the beautiful waters of the Emerald Coast.
